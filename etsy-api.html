Etsy API Integration

Application Overview

This application uses the Etsy Open API v3 to manage product listings for our Etsy shop um2design. The integration is developed and operated by um2 creative consulting UG (haftungsbeschraenkt), a registered German company.

Purpose

The API integration is used exclusively for:

  • Listing Management — Creating, updating, and managing digital product listings (design templates, color palettes, mood boards)
  • Inventory Sync — Keeping product availability in sync with our internal systems
  • Order Notifications — Receiving order data to trigger automated digital product delivery

The application is a private, internal tool. It is not a public-facing service, marketplace, or third-party app. It is used solely to manage our own shop's listings and operations.

Products

Our Etsy shop um2design offers digital interior design products created by Ulli Zelle, an interior designer with over 20 years of professional experience. Products include curated color palettes, room planning guides, and design templates — all delivered as digital downloads (PDF).
